Pootie's favorite anime character!

Nico Robin

Nico Robin is a member of Straw hat Pirates she is one of my favorite, Nico Robin use the power of hana-hana no mi which she can grow or manipulate part of her body like a flower. Also she is the best archaeologist.

Marco

Marco the Phoenix is the former 1st division commander of the Whitebeard Pirates. He ate a Mythical Zoan Devil Fruit that lets him turn into a phoenix, giving him blue flames that heal and allow him to fly.

Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro is the swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of Luffy’s most trusted crewmates, He is famous for his unique three-sword style, where he wields a sword in each hand and one in his mouth. Zoro wish is to become the world’s greatest swordsman, and he trains hard to achieve his goal. but he has zero sense on directions.

Why These Characters?

Nico Robin was chosen because she represents intelligence, mystery, and calm leadership. Her unique Devil Fruit power and role as an archaeologist make her a fascinating character.

Marco was included because he symbolizes resilience and loyalty. His healing flames represent protection and hope, making him a reliable ally.

Roronoa Zoro stands out for his strength, discipline, and determination to achieve his dream. His loyalty to Luffy and his crew shows true samurai spirit.
